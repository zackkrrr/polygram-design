Our story

Built from the ground up

Polygram Design was founded on the belief that design intent and construction reality should never diverge. Too many projects lose their precision somewhere between the architect's drawing and the contractor's site — we exist to close that gap.

Starting with a small team of designers and builders who had grown tired of the handoff failures typical in larger commercial projects, we built a practice where both disciplines share the same table, the same deadlines, and the same accountability.

Today, we operate across hospitality, retail, and workplace sectors, delivering projects where the geometry holds and the details matter.

Our mission

Precision at every stage

Our mission is straightforward: deliver commercial interiors that are designed with integrity and built without compromise. We don't believe in padding timelines, inflating scopes, or handing off blame.

We believe every project deserves a team that shows up with the same ambition on day one of construction as on the day the concept was approved. That discipline is what shapes the spaces we make.

What we stand for

Our values

Precision

Every dimension, every material, every joint. Precision is not an aesthetic choice — it is the baseline standard we hold ourselves to on every project.

Craft

We work with tradespeople who care about the finish as much as we do. Craft means being willing to redo something that isn't right, even when the client hasn't noticed yet.

Transparency

We communicate honestly at every stage — when the project is tracking well, and when it isn't. Our clients always know what's happening and why.
