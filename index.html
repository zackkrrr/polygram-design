Who we are

A contractor built

Polygram Design is a specialist contractor operating at the intersection of interior design and construction. We work with commercial clients to transform spaces that are functional, refined, and built to last.

From concept through completion, our in-house team handles every stage — eliminating handoff friction and delivering results that hold up to the precision of our plans.
